How FWRCFN Works

Our platform connects all stakeholders in the fight against food waste

Food Donors

Restaurants, hotels, and individuals can donate surplus food with easy scheduling and pickup.

Volunteers

Community members help collect and distribute food to community fridges and those in need.

Community Fridges

Strategic locations where donated food is stored and made available to those who need it.

Needy Users

People facing food insecurity can find nearby community fridges and request assistance.
